$$

\rightarrow
\Delta I=\frac{\Delta U}{R}
\Rightarrow
j\Delta S=\frac{E\Delta l}{\rho\frac{\Delta l}{\Delta S}}

j=\frac E \rho
\Rightarrow
j=\sigma E
